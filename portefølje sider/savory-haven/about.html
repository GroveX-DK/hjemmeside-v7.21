Our Story

Where It All Began

Founded in 2010, Savory Haven emerged from a dream to create a dining destination where culinary artistry meets warm hospitality. Our founder and head chef, Chef Michael Laurent, brought his Michelin-starred experience and passion for innovation to create a restaurant that celebrates both tradition and creativity.

From our humble beginnings as a small neighborhood bistro to becoming one of the city's most acclaimed restaurants, we've remained committed to our core values: exceptional quality, genuine hospitality, and culinary excellence.

Today, we continue to push boundaries while honoring the classics, sourcing the finest local and seasonal ingredients to craft dishes that tell a story with every bite.
